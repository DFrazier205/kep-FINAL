Our Story

Built on Expertise,
Driven by Excellence

Founded in 2017 in Bowie, KEP Event Planning is a full-service catering and event planning firm serving clients throughout the Washington metropolitan area. We specialize in delivering seamless, high-impact events for corporate, government, association, and private clients who expect precision, creativity, and flawless execution.

At the helm is a seasoned hospitality executive with more than 15 years of progressive leadership experience in catering, event sales, and large-scale operations. Our leadership combines strategic planning expertise with a deep understanding of client service, compliance, and operational excellence.

Our background includes directing multi-million-dollar catering operations with nationally recognized organizations including Aramark and Levy — exceeding quarterly sales goals, increasing beverage revenues by double digits, and building long-term client relationships that drive repeat and referral business.
